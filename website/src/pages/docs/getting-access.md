# Getting access

TimeCheese uses request-and-approve access: you sign yourself up, but an admin has to
approve you before you're in.

## How to get access

1. Download and open the app, then click **New here? Request access** on the sign-in screen.
2. Enter your email. You'll get a 6-digit verification code — enter it and choose a password.
3. You'll land on a **waiting for admin approval** screen. Ping your admin to approve you.
4. Once approved, click **Check again** and you're in. From then on, sign in with your email
   and password.

## FAQ

**Why the approval step?**

It's an internal tool — access is gated to the team, but no one has to manually create
accounts in Supabase anymore. The admin just flips a switch to approve you.
